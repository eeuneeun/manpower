About ManpowerGroup

ManpowerGroup® (NYSE: MAN) is the world’s workforce expert, creating innovative workforce solutions for more than 65 years.
As workforce experts, we connect more than 600,000 people to meaningful work across a wide range of skills and industries every day.
Through our ManpowerGroup family of brands – Manpower®, Experis™, Right Management® and ManpowerGroup® Solutions –
we help more than 400,000 clients in 78 countries and territories address their critical talent needs,
providing comprehensive solutions to resource, manage and develop talent.
In 2017, ManpowerGroup was named one of the World’s Most Ethical Companies for the seven consecutive year and
one of Fortune’s Most Admired Companies, confirming our position as the most trusted and admired brand in the industry.
