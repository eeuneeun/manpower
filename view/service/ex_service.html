Executive Search

Manpower Korea Executive Search Division focuses on the senior executive market and customized solutions for our clients.
We have 40 professional consultants & recruiters, understanding the needs of our clients, as they all have hands-on industry expertise and knowledge.

  Executive Search Division conducts over 1000 assignments and worked in partnership with hundreds of global clients to help for their business success.
We have placed numerous positions from mid-level to executive level, covering all segments of industries
as Automotive, Equipment & Machinery, Energy, Construction & Engineering, pharmaceutical & Bio, Medical Devices,
FMCG, IT, Semiconductor, Tourism & Hospitality, and Fashion & Retail

  Our process includes screening, advertising, assessing, shortlisting, and interviewing on your behalf,
so you see only the very best and most suitable candidates at the final interview stage
